About Neonexus

Name of the World: Neonexus

Neonexus is an expansive, tiered city stretching vertically more than horizontally. The lower layers are dense with towering skyscrapers interconnected by skybridges, creating a labyrinth of streets and alleys. Higher levels are accessible only to certain social classes or those with clearance, where the atmosphere is slightly clearer and sunlight occasionally filters through the artificial lights. At the base lies a network of old infrastructure where remnants of nature cling, but in higher levels, the city is washed in a sea of holographic lights and illuminated digital displays.

Centuries ago, the Earthâ€™s atmosphere was heavily polluted, blocking natural light and leading to what is now called the Perpetual Twilight. Neonexus was built in response to the growing need for hyper-connected, self-sustaining environments. Its layered construction was a social experiment to handle overpopulation and optimize technological advancements, but it also created a hierarchy where access to technology and resources became the new social divide.

Neonexus has no supernatural elements. However, advanced tech blurs reality with virtuality...
