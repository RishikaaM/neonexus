Central Conflict

The core tension in Neonexus is the battle between full digital immersion and reclaiming raw reality... If the Neonites succeed, reality will vanish entirely under layers of data. If the Untethered triumph, they could destabilize essential tech systems, plunging parts of the city into chaotic darkness.

Beliefs: Worship technological advancement... Neonites view Data Shades as remnants of the old world and hold “cleansing” rituals to purge lingering data from their devices, seeing them as purifiers.

Customs: Weekly gatherings, known as “The Flicker,” take place, where Neonites share device updates and virtual avatars as a way to enhance community bonds....

Beliefs: Oppose AR immersion, valuing tactile reality... They view Neonites with suspicion, often engaging in subtle acts of rebellion by blocking signals or sabotaging virtual layers.

Customs: Host “Bare Moments” gatherings... “Bare Moments” gatherings, where they disconnect and experience the physical world together. They also keep hidden maps of non-digital zones.
